How it works

Independent advisory, federally aligned process.

CapitalBridge is an independent funding facilitator. We curate active grant programs, verify eligibility, and route your file to matched reviewers — typically within 24–48 hours.

1. Discover

Browse our directory and filter by industry, country, or funding range. Each listing shows eligibility, required documents, funding band, and review time up front — no surprises.

2. Verify

Each application carries a one-time processing fee, paid by Bitcoin or bank transfer. The fee covers reviewer time, documentation help, and routing. Upload your proof of payment to unlock the application form.

3. Submit

Fill in the guided form — business details, contact, and a short use-of-funds narrative. Your file routes to matched reviewers and you'll hear back within 24–48 hours with next steps.

Important to know

CapitalBridge is an independent advisory. We are not a government agency or program. Processing fees are non-refundable once review begins. Funding decisions are made by the partner reviewers attached to each program.
